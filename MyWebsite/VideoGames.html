All about Video Games

I love to game and this page has some of my reccomendations!

Civilization 6

This game is amazing if you are trying to spend a whole afternoon. You begin as a civilization of your choice and work your way to victory throughout the years. Victory ranges from complete world domination or landing on Mars!You can even play with friends!

Hollow Knight

This game will absolutely charm your pants off. Character design, story, music, and world design make the game visually stunning and emotionally compelling. But don't let the cuteness fool you, this game has some difficult bosses and areas, enough to keep you engaged for hours upon end.

Leauge of Legends

I've been playing pretty consistantly for almost 3 years now and let me tell you, once you get past the somewhat toxic userbase, there is a game that is as fun as it is competitive. There can be a steep learning curve but the game is truly rewarding if you give yourself the chance to work towards getting better.

Stardew Valley

This game is like if you bottled up the smell of fresh apple pie baking in an oven and made a video game out of it. It is sweet, classic, and comforting. The game follows you as you inherit a farm and move to a small town from the big city. It's your job to bring the farm to it's former glory, while befriending the townsfolk and maybe even starting a family.

Valorant

Valorant is a competitive first person shooter. While I am absolutely terrible at first person shooters I genuinely enjoy this one. The character design is interesting the maps are fun and visually appealing. While the userbase is much like the userbase of League of Legends (The games are made by the same company) it can still be a rewarding experience especially if you do in fact like first person shooters.

Smash Bros Ultimate

This game is an absolute gem when it comes to party/family games. It's simple to pick up and play on the fly and always a lot of fun. But if you want to take more time to understand the intricate mechanics of each character you can be in for a very rewarding experience.

Animal Crossing New Horizons

This is on my MUST play games list for anyone. The characters, the gameplay, and the world around you border on so cute you might die. If you want a good time with ZERO negative experiences than play this game. Seriously, if you ever feel down or depressed this game can do wonders. Your animal villagers will always be there to be your best freinds and your quiet safe island will always be waiting.

Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild

This game is absolutely stunning. The open world is so massive and beautifully made you will think the $60 price tag was nothing. From hundreds of quests and a long overarching atory to follow you will never get bored.

Sea of Thieves

This game is so fun to play with friends! While there isn't much story the beauty of sailing on the ocean as a pirate will leave you wanting to rent a boat and try it yourself.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a classic. If you want a relaxing experience or a stressful one, this game can be played however way you want it to be.

Last of Us

While this game has it's gritty moments the story is phenomenal. If you want emotionally compelling characters in a post-apocolytic type world this game is for you.

Overwatch

This team based shooter is super fun game to play with friends. It has a competive streak but if you just want to have fun or even if you want to compete you can't go wrong with this game.
